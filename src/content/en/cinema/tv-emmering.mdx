The film encapsulates 125 years of TV-Emmering’s legacy, spanning from 1898 to the present day. Based upon an extensive 1,200-page club chronicle, this documentary distils pivotal events, esteemed club figures, and landmark achievements into a compelling visual narrative. Employing cinematic techniques, immersive animations, and a meticulously structured concept, the film masterfully translates the club’s rich history into a digestible and visually engaging experience.
